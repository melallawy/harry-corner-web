## Amazon PPC Structure

### Campaign 1: Sponsored Products — Auto Campaign (Discovery)

**Budget:** $10/day
**Bidding strategy:** Dynamic bids — down only

**Purpose:** Discover converting search terms. Run for 2-4 weeks, then harvest winners into manual campaigns.

**Negative keywords (add after 2 weeks):**
- whisk (too broad — will trigger balloon whisks, egg whisks)
- electric
- mixer
- stand mixer
