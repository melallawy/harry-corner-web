### Bid Strategy

| Campaign Type | Strategy | Notes |
|---------------|----------|-------|
| Auto | Dynamic bids — down only | Let Amazon find converting terms |
| Manual Exact | Dynamic bids — up and down | Aggressive on proven converters |
| Manual Phrase | Dynamic bids — down only | Conservative, harvest new terms |
| Manual Broad | Dynamic bids — down only | Discovery, low risk |
| Sponsored Brands | Fixed bids | Control spend, test headlines |
| Sponsored Brands Video | Dynamic bids — up and down | Video has higher CVR, bid up |

| Timeframe | Action |
|-----------|--------|

| Day 7 | Add irrelevant terms as negative keywords |
| Day 14 | Review performance, adjust bids |
| Day 14 | Pause keywords with 10+ clicks and 0 sales |
| Day 30 | Full audit — pause underperformers, scale winners |
| Ongoing | Weekly search term mining, bi-weekly bid adjustments |
