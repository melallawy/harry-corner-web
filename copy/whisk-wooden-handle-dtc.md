## FAQs

**Q: Is this Danish dough whisk sturdy enough for heavy sourdough dough?**
A: Yes. The three-loop stainless steel head is specifically designed for thick, stiff doughs. It won't bend or flex under heavy loads — that's the whole point. If your dough is dense enough to resist a wooden spoon, this whisk handles it.

**Q: What's the difference between a Danish and Swedish dough whisk?**
A: They're the same tool with different regional names. Both refer to a thick-wire whisk designed for bread dough. Our product uses both terms because bakers search for both — and the tool works exactly the same either way.

**Q: How do I clean the wooden handle dough whisk?**
A: Rinse under warm water immediately after use. The wide-loop design prevents dough from getting trapped. For deeper cleaning, wash with mild soap. Avoid soaking the wooden handle in water for extended periods to preserve its finish.

**Q: Is 13 inches the right size for my mixing bowl?**
A: 13 inches is the industry standard and works with most mixing bowls, Dutch ovens, and bread machines. It's long enough to reach the bottom of deep bowls without getting your hands into the dough.

**Q: Can I use this for pizza dough and other breads besides sourdough?**
A: Absolutely. While it's optimized for sourdough, this Danish dough whisk works beautifully for pizza dough, focaccia, cinnamon roll dough, biscuit dough, and any thick batter that would challenge a regular whisk or spoon.
