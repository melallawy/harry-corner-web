## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is 304 stainless steel really different from regular stainless steel?
Yes. 304 is a specific food-grade alloy (18% chromium, 8% nickel) that resists corrosion, bending, and rust far better than lower-grade alternatives like 430 stainless steel. It's the same material used in professional kitchen equipment, medical devices, and high-end cookware. When a whisk says "304 stainless steel," it's telling you exactly what you're getting — not hiding behind vague claims.

### Can this handle thick sourdough dough at high hydration?
Absolutely. The open-coil design is specifically engineered for high-hydration doughs (70%+). Unlike wooden spoons that clog or wire whisks that trap dough, the Harry Corner whisk lets dough flow through the loops — so you get a thorough mix without overworking your bread.

### Is it dishwasher safe?
Yes. The 304 stainless steel hook and wooden handle are both dishwasher safe. For everyday use, a quick rinse under warm water is usually enough. Dough slides off the open coil without scrubbing.

### What size is it?
