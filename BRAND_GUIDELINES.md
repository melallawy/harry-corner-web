# Harry Corner — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence
Premium kitchen tools that blend practical elegance with uncompromising durability. Tools, not gadgets.

## Logo
- **Wordmark:** HARRY CORNER (two-line stacked)
  - Line 1: HARRY (bold, 900 weight)
  - Line 2: CORNER (light, 300 weight)
- **Font:** Playfair Display (serif)
- **Color:** White on dark backgrounds

## Typography

| Usage | Font | Weight | Notes |
|-------|------|--------|-------|
| Headings (h1-h4) | Playfair Display | 700 Bold | Serif, elegant, -0.02em letter-spacing |
| Body text | Montserrat | 300 Light | Sans-serif, 0.01em letter-spacing |
| Buttons/Nav | Montserrat | 700 Bold | Uppercase, 0.15em letter-spacing |
| Taglines | Montserrat | 700 Bold | Uppercase, 0.25em letter-spacing, muted color |

## Color Palette

| Token | Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-------|-----|-------|
| --bg-primary | Black | `#121212` | Page background |
| --bg-secondary | Dark gray | `#1a1a1a` | Cards, sections |
| --bg-tertiary | Mid gray | `#2a2a2a` | Button backgrounds |
| --text-primary | White | `#ffffff` | Main text, headings |
| --text-secondary | Light gray | `#e2e2e2` | Body copy |
| --text-muted | Gray | `#8a8a8a` | Subtitles, secondary info |
| --border-color | Transparent gray | `rgba(138,138,138,0.3)` | Borders, dividers |
| --accent (gold) | Metallic gold | `#c9a96e` | Accent elements, hover states |

## Voice & Tone
- **Direct, confident, refined**
- Emphasize durability, craftsmanship, and professional-grade quality
- Avoid hype or gimmicks — let the product quality speak
- Tagline: *"Practical elegance. Durability you can trust."*

## Logo Usage
- Always use white/light logo on dark backgrounds
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of "HARRY"
- Do not stretch, rotate, or alter the logo proportions

## Photography
- Dark, moody, dramatic lighting
- Product-focused with shallow depth of field
- Authentic kitchen environments (not studio white)
- Images should feel warm and tactile

## Buttons

**Primary (Buy Now / CTA)**
- White background, dark text
- Pill-shaped (border-radius: 9999px)
- Uppercase, bold Montserrat, 0.15em letter-spacing
- Hover: slight lift (translateY -2px), shadow

**Secondary (Explore / Amazon)**
- Transparent background, white text, muted border
- Same pill shape and typography as primary
- Hover: border turns white, subtle background

## Spacing
- Section padding: 100px top/bottom
- Container max-width: 1200px, 24px side padding
- Card padding: 16px-20px
- Grid gap: 20px between product cards
