## Content Gaps

### What competitors aren't doing:
1. **Sourdough education content** — Most listings are purely transactional. Create how-to mixing guides.
2. **304 vs 430 stainless steel comparison** — No one explains why 304 matters. This is a trust-builder.
3. **Recipe integration** — Pair the whisk with specific sourdough recipes in A+ content.
4. **Cleaning demonstration** — Show dishwasher-safe vs. hand-wash in video/images.
5. **Hydration-specific messaging** — Reference 70%+ hydration doughs specifically (sourdough community language).

### Recommended content assets:
- Blog post: "Why 304 Stainless Steel Matters for Your Dough Whisk"
- A+ comparison chart: 304 SS vs 430 SS vs generic

- YouTube short: 30-second mixing demo showing no sticking
